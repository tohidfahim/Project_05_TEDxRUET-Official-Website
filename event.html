About

‘TEDxRUET: Changing Currents’ is a daylong event hosted by the Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology in its auspicious campus. The program will consist of invigorating and esoteric philosophical lectures on the changing prospects of our society and culture. Well-endowed speakers will promulgate their blissful ideas, research on diverse topics and concatenate visions for a better tomorrow. Issues such as, superbug, Bangabandhu-1 satellite, Henrietta Lacks, clean air achievement of Rajshai and other perceptive topics will be rigorously discussed and debated. Additionally, artists and performers with idiosyncratic talents will make their presence to entertain the audience. This event is open to all and encouraged to attend with friends and family. If you find yourself alone, you can also meet and greet people with similar interest during various ice-breaking sessions of the event. Come with an open mind and TEDxRUET will paint it will vibrant colors.
