TEDxRUET Changing Currents

15 February, 2019


Embellished by the rich heritage and cultural sensation of the Silk City Rajshahi (Bangladesh), TEDxRUET prompts to offer a unique experience unlike any other TEDx event before. With a core demography consisting of mostly inspiring, passionate and engaging youths, this event is focused towards populating the minds of these leaders of tomorrow; with new ideas, innovation and information. Whether you are a student, a teacher or a knowledge seeker, there are numerous insightful and engaging sessions for everyone. That being said, TEDxRUET welcomes your presence and aspires that you can discuss and discover effects of changing currents in your life. A dedicated and well-equipped TEDxRUET team of undergraduate students from various faculties of Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology is prepared to cater your every need and offer you a satisfying experience. Their hard work will adulated through your gratification.
